tls:
Download pfx after certificate is created in AKV

openssl pkcs12 -in <filename>.pfx -nocerts -nodes -out key.pem
Type: openssl pkcs12 -in <filename>.pfx -clcerts -nokeys -out cert.pem

kubectl create secret tls tls-secret --cert=<path/to-full-chain.crt> --key=<path/to.key>

Refs https://support.microfocus.com/kb/doc.php?id=7004039
